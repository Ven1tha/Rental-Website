Terms And Conditions

All The Answers You Need



INSURANCE
Terms for using Benson's Rentals Ltd’s self-drive hire policy:

i. All rentals are quoted on an individual basis with insurance charges applying to the specific authorised driver(s), A short insurance declaration form must be completed to obtain the quote.
ii. An Insurance Excess will apply. The amount of the excess depends on which car you have hired and is shown at the time of quotation /booking.
iii. The standard insurance excess ranges from $5,000 to $20,000. The security deposit held by us for the duration of the hire is taken to cover this insurance excess in the case of damage to the vehicle during the hire period. This can vary depending on risk assessment made by our insurers.
iv. If the driving license of the hirer/additional driver contains any current endorsements, these must be declared at the time of booking.
v. Only the person(s) named on the hire agreement are insured to drive the vehicle. If anyone other than the person(s) named on the hire agreement is found to be driving the vehicle, the security deposit will be considered a total loss. We may also seek prosecution for driving without insurance.



AGE AND LICENCE RESTRICTIONS
i. Prices are based on drivers being aged 35 to 70 years. However, insurance approval can be requested for drivers of any age. There is no guarantee that insurers will provide cover.
ii. All drivers must have held a full licence for a minimum of two years, must have no more than 50 current points on their NZ licence (equivalent of 6 Points in UK), and are subject to approval on completion of a insurance proposal form.
iii. Convictions for drink driving, driving under the influence of drugs; dangerous/careless driving within the past 5 years will not be accepted.
iv. Overseas licenses are accepted subject to the above conditions



BOOKING & CANCELLATION
i. We require a non-refundable booking fee to secure your booking. This is generally set at 20% of the Hire Fees but can vary.
ii. Cancellations within 7 days of the hire date will attract the full hire charge.
iii. When does the security deposit get refunded? This varies depending on your individual credit/debit card provider and their processing times. If there have been no damages, the refund will be processed from our team within 24 hours from the end of your hire date. Please note for bookings made within 14 days of hire date, the cancellation fee still applies as per terms above.



PRE-AUTHORISATIONS
i. Our team may carry out pre-authorisation checks for security purposes upon delivery of vehicles using various methods including our card payment terminals.
ii. The pre-authorisation is a temporary hold of the security deposit quoted upon your booking, this guarantees us that the funds are available to pay for any damages.
iii. When does the pre-authorisation get released? This varies depending on your individual credit/debit card provider and their processing times. If there have been no damages, the pre-authorisation release will be processed from our team within 24 hours from the end of your hire date.



EXCESS & SECURITY DEPOSIT
The excess and security deposit acts;
i. As a security bond
ii. As payments towards any mechanical damage which is not covered by our insurers, e.g., tyres, wheels, clutch, gearbox and any damage sustained during the hire period, excluding normal wear and tear, due to misuse and abuse.
iii. As an insurance excess against any accidental damage or loss caused to the vehicle by a third party or the hirer. The hirer is liable for the amount shown as Excess Deposit.
iv. Additional charges incurred during hire for excess mileage and/or, petrol reimbursement charges.
v. Please note: Security deposit refunds may take up to 2 weeks to process after hire/rental period.
